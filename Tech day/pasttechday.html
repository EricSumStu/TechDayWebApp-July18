Past Tech Days

Ericsson Ireland Technology Day took place on 14th September in our Software Campus, Athlone. “Visualize, Virtualize, Digitalize” was the theme for 2017 and was hosted jointly by John Griffin (Managing Director, Ericsson Ireland) and Mårten Pehrson (Head of PDU Network Analytics and Mgmt. & Athlone Site). The day was formally opened by Her Excellency Anna Brandt, Swedish Ambassador to Ireland. Ms. Brandt spoke about the unique business and cultural links between Ireland and Sweden. It was a great start to a great day.

The Technology Day plays an important role in cross-pollinating ideas and highlighting synergies between areas. 2017 was no different. In the area of automation, it was clear to see the links emerging between data & analytics & full-scale autonomics. Features such as Incident management are driving us further into the automation space. It was also great to see how our knowledge of network management has moved seamlessly into the 5G domain. In the area of continuous deployment & delivery, several demos showcased the parts that are coming together & will eventually allow us to deploy software continuously in our customer deployments. There was of course a strong link here with our emerging cloud deployments.

Last year, we had a Tech Talk from the GAA Official Technology Partner with Enda Finnan and Tomás Meehan. We learned the context of Ericsson technology partnerships in other sporting fields, the nature of the relationship with the GAA and the potential for technology innovation this partnership presents. We also got an insight into the technology challenges and vision for Croke Park and for the broader GAA. Overall, we had approximately 40 demos on the day. The overwhelming feeling was that there was just not enough time to take everything in. The winning demo was a very innovative one, which harnessed the model driven capabilities of ENM. The demo showed the integration of a Lego “node” on ENM proving conclusively that “ENM is Awesome”.
